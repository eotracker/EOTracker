Sources and Methodology:

Bill Status: Sourced from Congress.gov for sponsored bills; search of X.com for legislative intent.

Court Challenges: Search of X.com for lawsuits and rulings.

Risk Level: Score based on sum of 3 factors (Green=0-4, Yellow=5-7, Red=8-10):

1. Bill Status (0=Codified, 1=Sponsored, 2=Optional (within executive power however legislation will increase durability), 3=Intent declared, 4=No bill yet)

2. Court Challenges (0=None, 1=Threatened, 2=Lawsuit filed, 3=Negative ruling)

3. Impact Level (0=Low, 1=Medium, 2 or 3=High)

Impact Level: Assessment based on 3 factors; (0=Low=3 factors are Low; 1=Medium=1-2 factors Medium; 2=High=3 factors Medium, 3=High=1 or more factors High):

1. Population Impact (Low=<10k, Medium=10k-500k, High=>500k)

2. Economic Impact (Low=Less than $100M, Medium=$100M-$5B, High=Greater than $5B)

3. Longevity of EO Impact (Low=<1 years, Medium=1-5 years, High=>5 years)

Data sourced from public records; Risk and Impact ratings are subjective and may evolve. Court challenges reflect known filings or threats as of the last update. Contact: eotracker@proton.me
